Jean Grey Reid (1887-1956)

Biography

29th September, 1886

Birth, Bowerwalls, Barrhead, Renfrewshire, Scotland. Father, Wiliam Reid, mother, Janet F. Ferguson.

1891

Census:
Residence, 28 St. James St., Paisley, Renfrewshire, Scotland.
Occupation, Scholar.

31st March, 1901

Census:
Residence, 9 Ladt Lane, Paisley, Renfrewshire, Scotland. Occupation, Cotton Weaver.

24th October, 1918

Married George Birrell Irving, Scotland.

25th February, 1920

Birth of son, Thomas, Sydney, New South Wales, Australia.

27th December, 1923

Birth of son, William, paddingtony, New South Wales, Australia.

12th July, 1956

Death, Bexley, New South Wales, Australia, from Syncope, Coronary Declusion, Acute Gastritis.

13th July, 1956

Cremated Woronora Cemetery, Sutherland, New South Wales, Australia.
