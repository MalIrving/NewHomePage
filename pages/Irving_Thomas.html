Thomas Irving

abt 1855.

Born Kilpatrick-Flemming, Dumfrieshire, Scotland to parents Thomas Irving and Mary Gordon.

28th April 1873

Birth of son, Thomas, Newbie Barns, Annan, Dumfrieshire, Scotland.

abt. 1874

Birth of daughter, Johann Scott, Annan, Dumfrieshire, Scotland.

25th June 1875

Marriage to Mary Scott, Annan, Dumfrieshire, Scotland.

25th July 1877

Birth of daughter, Jane, Morningside, Annan, Dumfrieshire, Scotland.

13th April April 1879

Birth of son, Joseph, Lochfield, Annan, Dumfrieshire, Scotland.

3rd April 1881

Census.
Dwelling: Bellsprings, Annan, Dumfrieshire, Scotland.
Occupation: Quarryman.
Birthplace: Kirkpatrick-Flemming, Dumfrieshire, Scotland.

22nd June April 1882

Birth of daughter Mary, Bellsprings, Annan, Dumfrieshire, Scotland.

5th November 1883

Birth of son, George Birrell, Newbie Barns, Annan, Dumfrieshire, Scotland.

27th July 1884

Death, Blackhills's, Annan, Dumfrieshire, Scotland from Rheumatic fever, Pericarditis and Endocarditis.

Notes

No record of birth certificate, so could be in another parish or pre 1855. Marriage certificate: 25th June 1875 at Annan Parish Church, aged 20 years. Death certificate: 21st July 1884 at Blackhills, Annan aged 28 years, of Rheumatic fever, Pericarditis and Endocarditis.

He had different jobs and different addresses on his children's birth certificates. This was quite common at that time as servents and workman were selected at the yearly "hirings". He had been a farm servent, a ploughman and at his death a freestone quarryman (see back of town map re. quarry).

As Mary Scott had named her first illegitimate child Thomas Irving Scott, it looks as though he fathered it at 16 or 17 yrs, old. Also the baby was born at Newbie Barns, where Thomas's mother had lived.

Notes from Catherine Milburn.
