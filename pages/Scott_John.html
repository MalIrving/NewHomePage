John Scott (1826-1897)

Biography

24th January, 1826

Birth, Canobie, Dumfriesshire, Scotland. Father, George Scott, mother, Ann Taylor.

3rd April, 1829

Christening, Canobie, Dumfriesshire, Scotland.

17th December, 1847

Marriage to Mary Dalrymple, Scotland.

8th August, 1850

Birth of daughter, Jemima Ann, Dornoch, Landward, Sutherland, Scotland.

1851

Census: Residence, Foulsdyke, Landward, Dornock, Dumfriesshire, Scotland.

30th June, 1852

Birth of daighter, Mary, Newbie Barns, Dumfriesshire, Scotland.

3rd October, 1854

Birth of daughter, Janet Rae, Newbie Barns, Dumfriesshire, Scotand.

7th May, 1857

Birth of son, William, Annan, Dumfriesshire, Scotand.

8th May, 1859

Birth of daughter, John-Ann Rae, Annan, Dumfriesshire, Scotand.

1861

Census: Residence, Newbie Barnes, Dumfriesshire, Scotland.

13th May, 1862

Birth of Daughter, Elizabeth, Newbie Barns, Dumfriesshire, Scotand.

1871

Census: Residence, Newbie Barnes, Dumfriesshire, Scotland.

1881

Census: Residence, Newbie Barnes, Dumfriesshire, Scotland.

17th June, 1882

Death of wife, Mary from Uterine malignant tumour, Annan, Dumfriesshire, Scotand.

1889

Married Margaret George, Annan, Dumfriesshire, Scotand.

1891

Census: Residence, Annan, Dumfriesshire, Scotland.

20th November, 1897

Death, from cancer of the stomach, Annan, Dumfriesshire, Scotland.

Occupation at the time of death, Woodsman.

November, 1897

Buried Annan cemetery with 1st wife Mary and son William, Annan, Dumfriesshire, Scotand.
